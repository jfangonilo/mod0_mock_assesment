Class: Dog

Attributes:
energyLevel (integer)
hungerLevel (integer)
hairAmount (integer)
tired (boolean)
needBathroom (boolean)

Methods:
walk (reduces energyLevel, raises hungerLevel, chance to set needBathroom to false)
feed (reduces hunger level, sets needsBathroom to true)
playFetch (reduces energyLevel, sets tired to true)
brushHair (reduces hairAmount)
